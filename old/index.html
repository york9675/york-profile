I'm especially interested in civil aviation and the piloting side of it. I enjoy learning how airliners are operated, how crews manage complex flights, and the decisions behind safe, smooth operations.

I'm also an Airbus fanboy—from the fly-by-wire philosophy to the cockpit design, I love everything about it.

Flight simulator used: X-Plane 11 w/ ToLiss Airbus, you can check out my Volanta Profile for more details on my virtual flying activity.

Photography is a newer hobby for me. I'm a beginner and currently shoot with a Canon EOS R50.

I enjoy learning the basics, practicing composition, and capturing small moments as I explore more of the craft.
